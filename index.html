Specialist recruitment consultants

We are recruitment consultants based in Manchester, serving throughout the UK. We specialise in executive and management level roles in Digital, Marketing & Technology fields. We connect quality candidates with the right companies in various capacities, from Front End Development jobs to Search Engine Optimisation roles.

As an independent recruitment consultancy, we do not focus our business on sales targets like other agencies, we give full attention and insight into every placement, making sure its the perfect fit for both candidate and client.

We are unique due to our consultants being experienced in the industries and roles they are recruiting for. Having this hands-on knowledge helps us to connect the needs of candidates and employers.

What we do

We work hard to build long lasting relationships with our clients, we do this by fully understanding the roles they need to fulfil, the company ethos, culture and future direction.


Our industry expertise and direct experience helps us to understand candidates and their aspirations throughout their career journey and development.

What makes us different

You will benefit from a personal, flexible service from a company that puts quality over quantity every single time. We do not chase all available roles across all markets, by specialising in Digital, Technology, and Marketing we understand the skills you need and the talent you seek to recruit.

We screen every candidate over the course of many conversations and meetings. During this time, we come to understand the reasons for moving roles, career motivations, technical knowledge, background, and personality fit for your company. By approaching placements in this way, we aim to craft long-lasting relationships that stand the test of time.

Our fees are highly competitive, clear and transparent with no hidden costs or surprise charges. We confirm everything in writing before we begin any placement. Drop us a message on our contact form to see how we can help.

Our Values

We pride ourselves on our honesty, value, and thorough work. Our model is to build professional relationships cultivated over time and enhanced with positive results.
